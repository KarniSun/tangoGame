How to play
  • Fill every cell - tap to cycle empty → sun → moon.
  • Each row and column needs exactly 3 suns and 3 moons.
  • No more than 2 of the same touching in a line (no three in a row).
  • A = between two cells means they must match; a × means they must be opposite.
